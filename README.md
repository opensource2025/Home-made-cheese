### 7) health benefits of natural cheese:  
Natural cheese is not only delicious but also provides many health benefits:  

Rich in protein, which helps build and repair body tissues  

Contains calcium and phosphorus, essential for strong bones and teeth.  

Provides vitamins A, B2, and B12, which support vision, metabolism, and the nervous system.  

Helps in maintaining gut health due to natural probiotics found in fermented cheeses.  

When consumed in moderation, it contributes to a balanced and healthy diet
### 8) conclusion:  
Making natural cheese is an easy and educational process that shows how milk can be transformed into cheese through coagulation and separation.
It combines biology, chemistry, and food science in a simple experiment.
